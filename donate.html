Support CypherBay

CypherBay runs on hosting we pay for ourselves. No ads, no data broker deals, no premium tier holding features hostage behind a paywall. If it is useful to you and you want to help keep it free, independent, and running, here is how.

Pay in any coin, we receive XMR

Through Trocador's AnonPay, a no-KYC exchange aggregator, you can pay with almost any coin you already have: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, whatever is sitting in your wallet. Trocador converts it in the background and we only ever receive Monero (XMR) on our end. Trocador never holds your funds, requires no account, no email, and no ID at any step, and can be used entirely over Tor or I2P if you want zero metadata trail. It is not affiliated with CypherBay, we simply use their public payment link, and this is not a sponsored placement.
